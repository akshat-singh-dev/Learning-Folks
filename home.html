Welcome to Learning Folks

“Knowledge is power. Sharing knowledge is the key to unlocking that power.“

Our mission is to develop a community of learners adopting pedagogical strategies of student engagement and active learning. We are trying to evolve a platform to let the ewcomers teach in their area of expertise without any prerequisite.
